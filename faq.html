Frequently Asked Questions

Allure is your trusted destination for fashionable, affordable, and high-quality products. We offer a wide range of apparel, accessories, and more.

Placing an order is simple! Browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your billing details and confirm payment to place your order.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, and American Express) and PayPal.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use the tracking number on our website to monitor your shipment.
